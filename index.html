Questions you may have

Is it really unlimited changes and updates?

Yes. You can ask for how many changes and updates you need. And we don't charge any extra for it. We take care of it all for you so you're able to focus on building the business you love.

Should I expect any additional costs?

It’s good to budget for a domain name and for professional looking photos, if you don’t have them yet. A .com domain will go for around $10-20 per year. And the photographer can vary a lot depending on your area and their level of expertise.

Why don’t you just charge a one-off rate for the project?

We used to do that. But most clients benefit more from knowing that their website is always taken care of by an experienced person and that they don’t need to worry about dealing with any technical aspect of it. Besides, charging upfront often made the cost be an impediment for some small businesses that really needed our services.

Do you use Wordpress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow etc?

Although these platforms are great for entrepreneurs that manage their own website, you often need to forgo of customization control and website performance when using them. All of our websites are 100% custom-coded and that's to so we can make it look exactly the way we need and to ensure maximum performance.

Who owns the website after it's completed?

You own the domain name and the text content of the website. However, the website files (that include the design and the code) are essential for the ongoing service we provide, so they remain with us in case you decide to stop working together.

Why is there a minimum engagement of 6 months?

When your website goes live, you can already start seeing some results. But to get it to full speed and well-ranked on Google search results, it can take 6-24 months, since you’re competing with websites that have been around for years. So this minimum engagement is to give your website a chance to benefit you on the SEO aspect as well.
